Roads

Pick a road above (it lights up on the map). With the ROAD tool, each click drops the next point. UNDO PT removes the last one.

Exit nodes

With the EXIT tool, click where the exit should LEAD — the node anchors itself on the nearest interstate and builds its own ramps. Deleting a node takes its ramps with it.

Authored structures

With the PLACE tool, click to PIN this structure at exact coords — biomes stay procedural around it.

Exits & templates

AUTO-EXIT (v1) builds a bare ramp to a named town — the EXIT tool supersedes it. STAMP drops the chosen template at your last click.
